Amy L. Harrington M.D., C.P.E, F.A.P.A

Dr. Amy Harrington is Board-Certified in both General and Addiction Psychiatry. She has worked in a variety of treatment settings that span the socioeconomic spectrum. She is the Medical Director of the Acute Addiction Continuum at Community Healthlink, a community mental health center in Worcester, MA where there is a significant homeless and court-involved population. She was previously Associate Director for Physician Health Services, a program that works with doctors with mental health or substance use issues. She is the Chairperson for the Physician Health Committee at the University of Massachusetts Medical Center, where she is active on the faculty of the Medical School as a clinician, educator and researcher. Her research interests include management of Disruptive Physician Behavior, as well as the treatment of co-occurring substance use and serious mental illness.
